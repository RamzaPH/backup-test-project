Bayugo-Dichoso

Optometric Clinic

The Bayugo-Dichoso Optometric Clinic (BDOC) started its roots in Carmona, Cavite during the late 1970's as a part of the Bayugo Polyclinic which was comprised by the Vargas-Bayugo siblings, who all of which are doctors with different specializations. Now with 45 years in the industry, we continue to pursue our mission to promote health and wellness through the eyes of our clients.
