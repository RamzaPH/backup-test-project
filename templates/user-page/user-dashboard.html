Eye Health Tips

  • Take Regular Breaks

    Follow the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to reduce eye strain.

  • Protect Your Eyes from UV Rays

    Always wear sunglasses with 100% UV protection when outdoors to prevent damage from harmful rays.

  • Stay Hydrated

    Drinking plenty of water helps maintain moisture in your eyes and prevents dryness.

  • Eat Eye-Friendly Foods

    Include foods rich in vitamins A, C, and E, as well as omega-3 fatty acids, to support eye health.
